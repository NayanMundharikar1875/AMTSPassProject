About AMTS

AMTS is India's largest Municipal Transport Service for local conveyance. It is a voluntary service managed by Amdavad Municipal Corporation under Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations Act. Amdavad Municipal Transport Service (AMTS) runs the public bus service in the city of Amdavad. The responsibility of the administration of AMTS comes under the Amdavad Municipal Corporation.

The Beginning

There existed three transport services viz. ABC Co. (Amdavad Bus Corporation), Morris Transport and Munshi Bus service, before the municipal bus service. There was shortage of petrol till 1946 due to Second World War and the petrol supply was in limited quantity even in 1947 when the municipal bus service started. Coal-gas was used as fuel earlier in the buses, many buses were plying on gas.

There were approximately 50,000 commuters who traveled in such buses, Morris company operated some 32 bus routes in the city. The buses ran on Gandhi Road, and Relief Road from Bhadra in city area. There were clock-wise and anticlock-wise routes running from shahpur to shahpur.

Minimum bus fare was one Anna (6paise), and Maximum bus fare was three Annas (20 paise). They were in force from 1-1-47.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the operating hours of AMTS buses?

AMTS buses typically operate from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily. However, some routes may have different schedules. You can check specific route timings on our timetable page or mobile app.

How can I purchase a monthly pass?

Monthly passes can be purchased at any AMTS pass office or authorized agents. You'll need to provide ID proof and a passport-sized photograph. Student passes require additional documentation from your educational institution.

What documents are required for a student pass?

For student concession passes, you need:

  • Student ID card or bonafide certificate from your institution
  • Passport-sized photograph
  • Address proof
  • Previous pass (for renewal)

How do I file a complaint about bus service?

You can file complaints through:

  • Our 24x7 helpline: 079-25391807
  • Email: complaints@amts.co.in
  • In-person at any AMTS office
  • Using the feedback form on our website
Please provide details like bus number, route, time, and nature of complaint for faster resolution.

Are there any concessions for senior citizens?

Yes, senior citizens (age 60+) are eligible for:

  • 50% discount on all passes
  • Priority seating on buses
  • Special assistance at major bus stands
Valid age proof must be presented when purchasing passes or boarding buses.
