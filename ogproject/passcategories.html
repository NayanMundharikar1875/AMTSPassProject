Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AMTS passes

How can I purchase an AMTS pass?

You can purchase AMTS passes through:

  • AMTS website (online payment)
  • AMTS head office and major bus stands
  • Authorized pass selling agents

What documents do I need for a student/senior citizen pass?

For student passes you need:

  • School/college ID card
  • Bonafide certificate (for first-time applicants)

For senior citizen passes you need:

  • Proof of age (Aadhar card, passport, etc.)
  • Passport size photograph

Can I get a refund if I don't use my pass?

AMTS passes are non-refundable once issued. However, in exceptional cases like medical emergencies, you may apply for a refund at the AMTS head office with proper documentation.

What should I do if I lose my pass?

Immediately report the loss at the nearest AMTS office or call our helpline. You may need to purchase a new pass, but we can block the lost pass to prevent misuse.
